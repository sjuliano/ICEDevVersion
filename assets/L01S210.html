Identifying Strategies to Avoid Obstacles

The hiring process should begin long before a position is open. The following tips are activities that you should be completing at all times. They will help you avoid the most common obstacles to the hiring process.

  • Keep up a working relationship with your HR Office or representative. Pay attention to announcements and communications from them and interact with them when possible.
  • Keep in touch with your subordinates and peers so you are aware of possible future vacancies.
  • Become familiar with the overall recruiting, screening, and hiring process. Look for training opportunities that help you with your responsibilities within the hiring process.
