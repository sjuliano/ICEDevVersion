Introduction:

The Department of the Interior Has Significant Hiring Demands… and Hiring Managers Have a Critical Role in the Process.

The Department of the Interior has a workforce of 80,156 employees and hired 14,890 employees in the third quarter of FY 2011 alone.

OPM recently mandated that new Federal Government hires take no more than 80 days. The DOI is streamlining and improving its hiring process in response. As a Hiring Manager, it is critical that you understand your role and responsibilities associated with OPM’s 80-day hiring.
