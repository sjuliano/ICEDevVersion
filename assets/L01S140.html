Step 10. Review Applications, Schedule and Conduct Interviews, Check References, Make Selection, and Return Certificate

Who: Hiring Manager

What: Review applications, schedule and conduct interviews, check references, make the selection, and return the certificate of eligibles that identifies whom you have selected for the position.

It is critical to follow your office’s standard operating procedures (SOPs) for all of the actions required during this step. SOPs must be in compliance with the appropriate laws and regulations.

Ask your HR Office to review them with you to ensure full compliance.

How Long: 15 days
