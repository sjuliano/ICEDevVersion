Welcome to the DOI University’s course on the OPM Federal Hiring Process!

This course is intended for Federal Hiring Managers. It describes OPM’s 80-day Federal Hiring Process and explains the Hiring Manager’s responsibilities in the process. The course uses video scenarios, text, graphics, and animation to illustrate the important points that Hiring Managers need to know about the process. Participants are required to complete a short assessment at the end of the course.

The course should take you between 15-20 minutes to complete. Navigate through the course by selecting the Next button at the bottom of the screen. When you encounter a video, select the Play button on the bottom left corner of the video window. Additional resources, such as documents and additional information, can be accessed by selecting the Info button on the bottom left of the screen.

The objectives of this course are:

  • Identify key steps in the 80-day Federal Hiring Process
  • Identify key decisions you should make before posting the job vacancy
  • Identify strategies for avoiding obstacles in the hiring process
